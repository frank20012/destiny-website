Help Center

Frequently asked questions

Find answers to common questions about accounts, wallet funding, services, dashboard access, and platform usage.

How do I create an account?

Go to the sign up page, fill in your details, and submit the form to create your account.

How do I sign in?

Use the sign in page with your registered email and password to access your dashboard.

How do I fund my wallet?

Open the wallet page, choose your amount, select a payment method, and proceed with funding.

Where can I see my orders?

Your orders page contains your order history, statuses, and recent activity.

Can I use the API?

Yes. The API page provides your key, usage summary, and endpoint examples for integration.

Does the site support dark mode?

Yes. The platform supports light and dark themes, with light mode set as the default.

Can I update my account details?

Yes. Open the settings page to edit your profile, security, and account preferences.

Is the platform mobile responsive?

Yes. The pages are designed to adapt to desktop, tablet, and mobile screens.

Still need help?

Reach out through the contact page and we’ll guide you.
