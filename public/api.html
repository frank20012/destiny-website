FAQ

Common questions

Do your numbers work for WhatsApp, Telegram, Instagram? +
Yes! Our virtual numbers work with all major services including WhatsApp, Telegram, Instagram, Gmail, Twitter/X, Netflix, Coinbase, and hundreds more. They are real phone numbers, not temporary SMS services.
How fast does the OTP arrive? +
Typically under 3 seconds. Our platform uses WebSockets to push SMS messages to your inbox the moment Twilio receives them — no manual refreshing needed.
Can I keep a number permanently? +
Yes! On the Pro and Reseller plans, numbers can be rented permanently and renew automatically each month. On the Starter plan, numbers are rented for 30 days.
Can I start a reseller business with NumVault? +
Absolutely. Our Reseller plan gives you full API access, webhook support, and the ability to set your own prices. Many of our customers run their own profitable virtual number businesses on top of NumVault.
Do you offer numbers for Nigeria? +
Yes! We offer Nigerian numbers and our platform is fully accessible from Nigeria. We also support international numbers (US, UK, Canada, Germany, etc.) which are popular among Nigerian users for OTP verification.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, and major cryptocurrencies. All payments are processed securely via Stripe.
